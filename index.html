Est. 1998

Seattle’s Only
Country Bar

For over 25 years, The Little Red Hen has been the beating heart of country music in the Pacific Northwest. Nestled in Seattle’s Green Lake neighborhood, we’re a honky-tonk dive bar with a soul as big as the open range.

From live bands to line dancing, bluegrass jams to karaoke nights — there’s something happening every single night. We’ve hosted legends, launched local acts, and even had Stone Temple Pilots film their “Plush” music video right on our stage.

Strong drinks, affordable prices, great food, and a dance floor that never sleeps. Come as you are — boots optional.
